About Me

PhD candidate in Astrophysics at the University of Bologna (Italy). I am currently working at the Istituto di Radioastronomia (INAF-IRA, Bologna, Italy). I like to observe non-thermal phenomena in radio galaxies and galaxy clusters, especially if something is going on with magnetic fields.
