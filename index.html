About Me

PhD candidate in Astrophysics at the University of Bologna (Italy), working at the Istituto di Radioastronomia (INAF-IRA, Bologna, Italy). I like to observe non-thermal phenomena in radio galaxies and galaxy clusters at different radio wavelenghts.

Together with this, I am currently working in HPC and GPU enabling of softwares for radio interferometric imaging.

I love sports (huge football and tennis fan), music, and crime novels.

Skills

Programming languages and interfaces

  • C/C++, Python, Bash, CWL, Fortran, IDL, definetly *not* HTML (as you can easily guess from this website...)

  • MPI, OpenMP, CUDA, HIP, for parallelization and GPU offloading on multiple architectures; NVIDIA HPC-SDK, ROCm, and profiling tools (such as NVIDIA Nsight)

Software

  • CASA data calibration and imaging using VLA data (both total intensity and polarization)

  • Capture and SPAM pipeline for uGMRT
